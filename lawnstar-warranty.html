🇦🇺 Australian Consumer Law (ACL) Rights
Our returns policy is in addition to your rights under the Australian Consumer Law. If a product has a major fault, you are entitled to a refund, replacement, or repair — regardless of the time since purchase. These rights cannot be excluded, restricted, or modified.

Under ACL, a product has a major failure if it is substantially unfit for purpose, unsafe, or significantly different from the description. For minor failures, we will repair the product within a reasonable time or offer a replacement.
